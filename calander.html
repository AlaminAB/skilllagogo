About The Course

Love the look of watercolor but not sure where to start? Beginning anything can feel daunting, and watercolor seems to have a reputation for being intimidating. But it doesn’t have to be! Join us for a workshop that’s all about those first steps into the colorful, quirky world of watercolor. Learn basic classic techniques, learn some more modern applications, and tackle your fear with abstract experiments. You’ll need a basic watercolor set, brushes, and paper. This is a judgment-free zone so you can feel safe and comfortable sharing your first watercolor paintings with the group!

About The Teaching Partner

Vira Sketcher is the founder artist at Amore Creations. She is a highly creative multitalented Singaporean with a passion to make a difference to society by using beautiful forms of art and crafts. She has successfully led and conducted several projects, workshops and events across Singapore. Vira, who won the Best Designing Performance award from the Edit Institute in Singapore, is skillful in making authentic, bright and colourful batik paintings, plaster cast sculpting, hand embroidery and oil painting. She also masters 3D Character Animation and graphic designing.
